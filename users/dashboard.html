About

iam.Bakersbakes is home-based bakery in Kuching, Sarawak, founded by a 23-year old dedicated baker with a talent for transforming everyday cravings into extraordinary creations offering a large selection of cakes, tarts, and pastries. The bakery has gained recognition for its individualised approach to baking since opening in early 2021. The origin of iam.Bakersbakes is what sets it apart, from self-taught baker to business owner is a monument to her inventiveness and commitment, and it started with a personal craving for burnt cheesecake.
